Our Story at A Glance

Project ECO-TALES: Environmental Campaign on Transmitting Awareness and Lessons through Environmental Stories is a project by the Centrexians of the 25th Center for Excellence (CENTREX) for Research and Student Leadership from Lagro High School. Their creative minds coupled with their unwavering care for our environment are the driving force as to why this project was conceived.

Project ECO-TALES is an online website which is loaded with stories about the environment. Its primary goal is to spread awareness regarding the pressing issues our world experiences in general. Furthermore, with this initiative, the proponents aim not only to spread awareness about the environment but improve students’ literacy rates as well.
