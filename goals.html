Our Goals

Mission

  Project ECO-TALES aims to transmit awareness on the issues concerning the environment in a fun and interactive way. Through this, we aspire to be the vanguards of change through utilizing technology. It is our pursuit to have a Lagronian community with a revitalized environmental literacy and a renewed commitment in helping to save the environment.

Vision

  We strive to be the stepping stone in revolutionizing the campaign for environmental literacy in the country by starting at the Lagronian community and hopefully be cascaded throughout Quezon City, and eventually throughout the whole nation.
