Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

When was EcoTales founded?

  A group of passionate students from Lagro High School's 25th batch of the Center of Excellence (CENTREX) for Research and Student Leadership Special Edition launched EcoTales on October 23, 2023, with the guidance of their school's Secondary Learner Government (SSLG), their advisers, and administrators.

Are the storybooks available offline?

  Yes, the storybooks are available offline. You can download them and read them without an internet connection.

How do I download the storybooks?

  Simply click on the "Download" button on the bottom panel of the story you want to read. The story will then be downloaded to your device as a PDF file.

What age is allowed to read?

  EcoTales storybooks are for all ages. The stories are written in a way that is engaging and informative for both children and adults.

Why should we answer the assessments?

  We encourage you to answer the assessments before and after you read the story as it helps us track your reading comprehension and identify areas where you may need additional support. Additionally, your responses would help us improve the quality of our storybooks.

How can I contribute to EcoTales?

  We are always looking for new stories and illustrations to add to our website. If you have a story or illustration that you would like to share with us, please contact the SSLG.

What are the benefits of reading EcoTales storybooks?

  There are many benefits to reading EcoTales storybooks. Our storybooks may help children:

  • Learn about the importance of the environment
  • Develop a love of reading
  • Improve their reading comprehension skills
  • Become more aware of environmental issues
  • Learn how to take action to protect the environment

What is the purpose of EcoTales?

  The purpose of EcoTales is to educate and inspire children and adults about the importance of protecting the environment. We believe that storytelling is a powerful tool for promoting environmental awareness and action.

How are the EcoTales storybooks created?

  The EcoTales storybooks are created by a team of volunteers, including writers, illustrators, and educators. We work together to develop stories that are engaging, informative, and inspiring.

What are the EcoTales assessments?

  The EcoTales assessments are designed to measure your understanding of the material in our storybooks. We encourage you to take the assessments before and after reading the stories to track your progress over time.

How can I use EcoTales storybooks in my classroom?

  The EcoTales storybooks can be used in a variety of ways in the classroom. You can use them to:

  • Teach about environmental issues
  • Promote reading comprehension skills
  • Encourage critical thinking
  • Inspire students to take action to protect the environment

What are some other ways I can help protect the environment?

  There are many things you can do to help protect the environment. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle
  • Conserve water and energy
  • Use environmentally friendly products
  • Support sustainable businesses
  • Get involved in environmental activities and programs here in Quezon City
